About GV Cars

Reliable Rides. Comfortable Journeys.

GV Cars is a trusted car rental service based in Chennai, founded by Suneetha Nagalapuram. We are committed to making travel simple, reliable, and affordable for everyone — whether it is a short city ride or a long-distance trip.

We understand how important it is to arrive on time, travel in comfort, and feel safe throughout your journey. That is why every vehicle in our fleet is well maintained and every driver is experienced and verified.

From daily commutes and airport transfers to corporate travel and outstation trips, our flexible rental plans are designed to suit all kinds of travel needs.

How to Book

Ready in Just a Few Steps

Booking a car with GV Cars is quick and simple. Here is how it works.

Step 01

Make a Reservation

Call us or chat with us via WhatsApp or live support to make your advance booking. Our team will help you pick the right vehicle.

Step 02

Pick Up the Car

Collect your vehicle from our pickup point, or choose doorstep delivery and we will bring it to your preferred location on time.

Step 03

Drive Yourself

Take the wheel and enjoy your journey with family or friends. Our cars are clean, well maintained, and ready to go.

Step 04

Enjoy Your Trip

Have a great journey. Once you return the car, any applicable refund will be processed straight away — no fuss.
